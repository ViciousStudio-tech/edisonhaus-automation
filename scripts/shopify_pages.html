Shipping & Delivery

Everything you need to know about getting your order to your door.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Estimated Delivery Times

Region Standard Shipping Express Shipping
United States 7–15 business days 5–10 business days
Canada 10–20 business days 7–14 business days
Europe / UK 10–25 business days 7–15 business days
Rest of World 15–30 business days 10–20 business days

Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to customs processing or carrier delays. We are not responsible for delays caused by customs or local postal services.

Shipping Costs

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $35. Orders under $35 have a flat shipping fee of $4.99.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this to monitor your package on the carrier's website. If tracking has not updated after 7 days, please contact us and we will investigate.

Returns & Exchanges

Not happy with your order? We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Contact support@edisonhaus.store to start a return. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the item.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things customers ask most.

Where do your products come from?

We source products from vetted international suppliers who specialize in home decor, lighting, kitchen, and lifestyle goods. Every item is selected for quality, design, and value before it appears in our store.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping to the US takes 7–15 business days. Express options are available at checkout. For full details, visit our Shipping & Delivery page.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! All orders over $35 ship free with standard delivery. Orders under $35 have a flat $4.99 shipping fee.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. You will receive an email with your tracking number as soon as your order ships. If you do not see it, check your spam folder or contact us.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return window for unused items in their original packaging. Just email support@edisonhaus.store and we will walk you through the process. Refunds are issued within 5–7 business days after we receive your return.

My item arrived damaged. What do I do?

We are sorry to hear that. Please email us a photo of the damaged item within 48 hours of delivery and we will send a replacement or issue a full refund — no return needed.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Delivery times and any customs duties vary by destination. Check our Shipping & Delivery page for regional estimates.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@edisonhaus.store. We respond within 24 hours, Monday through Saturday.

Did not find your answer? Reach out to us directly and we will be happy to help.
